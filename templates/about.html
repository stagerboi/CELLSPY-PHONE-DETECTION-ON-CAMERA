About CellSpy

CellSpy is a real-time phone detection system that monitors camera feeds and triggers an alarm whenever a phone is detected in restricted areas. Using the powerful YOLOv8 model with Python, it ensures strict enforcement of no-phone policies in classrooms, offices, and other secure locations.
